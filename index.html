EventCast helps you discover events you care about. We collect and use only the information necessary to provide and improve our services, always with your privacy in mind.

Usage & Diagnostics

  • Searches, event views, likes/saves, RSVP actions.

Location (with permission)

Content You Provide

2) How We Use Your Information
