Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains how we handle data collected to provide and improve EventCast.

Information We Collect How We Use It Sharing & Disclosures Storage & Security Data Retention Location Data Children's Privacy International Transfers Changes

1) Information We Collect

Account & Profile

  • Name and email (passwords are stored hashed).
  • Optional profile info (photo, interests, home city).

Usage & Diagnostics

  • Searches, event views, saves, RSVP actions.
  • Device data (OS, model), app/version, crash logs, performance metrics.

Location (with permission)

  • Approximate or precise location to surface nearby events and recommendations.

Content You Provide

  • Reviews, comments, photos, support requests and feedback.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features: event discovery, RSVP, personalization.
  • Improve relevance and product quality.
  • Send important notices (policy updates, security alerts).
  • Maintain safety, detect abuse and prevent fraud.

3) Sharing & Disclosures

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, notifications) under data protection terms.
  • Event organizers when you RSVP/register, to manage attendance or send event-specific updates.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4) Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest where supported.
  • Access limited to authorized personnel; industry-standard security controls are used.

5) Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as needed to provide the service and to meet legal obligations. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6) Children’s Privacy

EventCast is not directed to children under 13 (or applicable age). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7) International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than where you live. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted here; please review periodically.
